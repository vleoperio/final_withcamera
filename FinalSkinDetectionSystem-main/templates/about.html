About our System

Our skin disease detection system for eczema and acne is a cutting-edge technology designed to accurately diagnose and monitor these common skin conditions. Using advanced image recognition algorithms, our system can analyze photos of the affected skin and provide a detailed assessment of the severity and type of eczema or acne present. With the ability to detect subtle changes in the skin, our system can help users track the progression of their skin conditions over time and provide personalized recommendations for treatment and skincare routines. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, our system offers a fast and reliable way to identify and manage eczema and acne, ultimately improving the overall health and appearance of the skin. Whether you are struggling with chronic eczema flare-ups or persistent acne breakouts, our skin disease detection system is here to support you on your journey to clearer, healthier skin.
